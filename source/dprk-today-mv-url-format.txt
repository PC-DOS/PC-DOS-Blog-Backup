[[code]]
https://dprktoday.com/content/movie/flv_mv/vod/dprktoday/kor_tv/{{YEAR}}/{{DATE}}-uri-q.mp4
[[/code]]

{{@@{{YEAR}}@@}}为年份，格式为YYYY。

{{@@{{DATE}}@@}}为日期，格式为YYYYMMDD。

例如：

[[code]]
https://dprktoday.com/content/movie/flv_mv/vod/dprktoday/kor_tv/2019/20190501-uri-q.mp4
[[/code]]

-----

有时会在主文件名和扩展名之间出现一个空格（{{%20}}），成为以下格式：

[[code]]
https://dprktoday.com/content/movie/flv_mv/vod/dprktoday/kor_tv/{{YEAR}}/{{DATE}}-uri-q%20.mp4
[[/code]]

{{@@{{YEAR}}@@}}为年份，格式为YYYY。

{{@@{{DATE}}@@}}为日期，格式为YYYYMMDD。

例如：

[[code]]
https://dprktoday.com/content/movie/flv_mv/vod/dprktoday/kor_tv/2019/20190501-uri-q%20.mp4
[[/code]]
