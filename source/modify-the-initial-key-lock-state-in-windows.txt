注册表路径:

[[code]]
HKU\.DEFAULT\Control Panel\Keyboard\
[[/code]]

DWORD值{{InitialKeyboardIndicators}}控制系统开机时三个锁定键，即{{CapsLock}}、{{NumLock}}和{{ScrollLock}}的初始状态

||~ 可用的设定值(十进制) ||~ 含义 ||
||= 0 || 关闭所有键锁定 ||
||= 1 || 仅开启{{CapsLock}} ||
||= 2 || 仅开启{{NumLock}} ||
||= 3 || 开启{{CapsLock}}与{{NumLock}} ||
||= 4 || 仅开启{{ScrollLock}} ||
||= 5 || 开启{{CapsLock}}与{{ScrollLock}} ||
||= 6 || 开启{{NumLock}}与{{ScrollLock}} ||
||= 7 || 开启全部键锁定 ||
